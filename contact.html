FAQ

Common questions, honest answers

How quickly will I hear back?

Our team responds within 3 business hours. For urgent issues, use live chat in-app or call us directly.

Is there a free trial?

Yes — every plan comes with a 14-day free trial, full access, no credit card required.

Can I book a live demo before signing up?

Absolutely. Select "Book a product demo" in the form or click "Book a demo" in the hero — a product specialist will schedule a personalised walkthrough.

Do you offer discounts for startups or non-profits?

Yes — 40% off for early-stage startups and 60% off for registered non-profits. Reach out via the form to apply.

How do I cancel my subscription?

Cancel anytime from your account settings with one click — no calls or forms. Access continues until end of billing period.
